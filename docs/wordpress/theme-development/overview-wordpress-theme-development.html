Overview WordPress Theme Development

About WordPress Themes

WordPress Themes are files that work together to create the design and functionality of of a WordPress site. Each Theme may be different, offering many choices for site owners to instantly change their website look.

Why WordPress Themes?

You may wish to develop WordPress Themes for your own use, for a client project or to submit to the WordPress Theme Directory.

Why else should you build a WordPress Theme?
  • To create a unique look for your WordPress site.
  • To generate diffrent website results and looks
  • To quickly switch between two site layouts, or to take advantage of a Theme or style switcher to allow site owners to change the look of your site.
Why should you build your own WordPress Theme?
  • It's an opportunity to learn more about HTML, CSS and PHP.
  • It's an opportunity to put your expertise with CSS, HTML, and PHP to work.
  • It's creative and fun.
  • If you release it to the public, you can feel good that you shared and gave something back to the community (okay, bragging rights)
